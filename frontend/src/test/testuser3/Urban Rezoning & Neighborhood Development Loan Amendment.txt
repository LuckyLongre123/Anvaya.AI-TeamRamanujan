PROPOSAL: Urban Rezoning & Neighborhood Development Loan Amendment
DATE: February 03, 2014
PROJECT ID: Denver-2014-13-1004

1. PROJECT OBJECTIVE

To facilitate the transition of property zoning from Residential Mixed Use (R-MU) to Suburban Mixed Use (S-MX) and provide financial backing for affordable housing via Del Norte Corporation.

2. SCOPE OF WORK

- Zoning Reclassification: Transition of properties (e.g., 8822 Bee, 1205-1275 Osage St).
- Bulk Plane Limitation Audit: Ensuring building heights comply with the omnibus package.
- Loan Extension: Increase existing loan to Del Norte Neighborhood Development Corp by $1,000,000.

3. BUDGET & FUNDING

- General Government Special Revenue Fund: $1,000,000 (Amendment)
- Franchise Renewal Fees: $2,000,000 (Allocated for City Infrastructure)

4. COMPLIANCE & LEGAL

Must adhere to Denver Zoning Code and Long Beach Municipal Code Chapter 21.54 regarding billboard restatements and public signage. Failure to pass these ordinances will result in a lapse of building limitations and potential legal challenges from developers.
